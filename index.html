About Me


Heather Smith is a full stack developer currently enrolled in Georgia Tech's Full Stack Flex Bootcamp. Her skills include HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. She also has a strong understanding of design, color theory, and composition.

Before the pandemic, Heather was working as an office administrator for a small label printing company. Since experts advised everyone to stay home, she did just that and started self-guided studies with Codecademy. After studying through the summer and fall, it seemed like she had a foundation for coding. It was time to move forward. So she enrolled in the bootcamp in hopes of starting a new career when it is safe to do so.

When she isn't coding, Heather likes gardening, hiking, cooking, thrift shopping, and video games. Currently, she lives in Sandy Springs, Georgia with her husband and two cats.
